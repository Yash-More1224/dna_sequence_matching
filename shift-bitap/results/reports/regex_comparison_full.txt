EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
----------------------------------------

For APPROXIMATE matching: Shift-Or/Bitap wins decisively

Python's re module CANNOT efficiently perform k-error matching.
Shift-Or/Bitap provides native O(n*m*k) approximate matching.

RECOMMENDATION:
For DNA sequence analysis requiring mutation tolerance,
